This Privacy Policy explains how Tally (“the app”, “we”, “us”), developed by Saksham Bhatt, handles your information. Tally is a game-night score-tracking app. By using Tally you agree to this policy.

Tally is offline-first: it works fully without an account, and your data stays on your device. Signing in is optional and only used to back up and sync your data across devices.

Information we collect

1. Account information (only if you sign in). If you choose to sign in with Google Sign-In, we receive from Google your email address, a unique Google account identifier, and your name and profile photo as provided by your Google account. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (a Google service). We do not see or store your Google password.

2. Content you create. The game data you enter — circle (group) names, player names, the games you track, and session results and scores — is stored on your device. If you are signed in, this content is also synced to Google Cloud Firestore so it is backed up and available on your other devices.

3. App preferences. Your settings (for example, light/dark theme) are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us.

We do NOT collect: your precise or approximate location, your contacts, advertising identifiers, health data, or payment information. Tally contains no ads and uses no third-party advertising or analytics/tracking SDKs.

How we use information

  • To provide core features — tracking sessions, leaderboards, statistics, and rivalries.
  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in (account information).
  • To back up your content and sync it across your devices (only when signed in).

We do not use your information for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell it.

Where your data is stored & third-party services

When signed in, your account information and content are processed and stored by Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) on Google’s servers. Their handling of data is governed by the Firebase Privacy and Security terms and the Google Privacy Policy. If you never sign in, your data remains on your device and is not sent to any server.

A note on player names. Names you add for other players are content you choose to enter. Please avoid entering other people’s sensitive personal information. You are responsible for the content you add to your circles.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Data is shared only with Google Firebase as our infrastructure provider, solely to deliver authentication and sync, and where required by law.

Security

Data transmitted between the app and Firebase is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we rely on Google’s infrastructure protections for data stored in the cloud.

Your choices, data retention & deletion

  • In-app deletion: you can delete circles, members, games, and sessions within the app at any time. Deleting synced content removes it from your cloud backup.
  • Local data: uninstalling the app, or clearing its storage in your device settings, removes all data held on the device.
  • Account & cloud data deletion: to delete your account and all associated data stored in the cloud, email us at bhattsaksham0311@gmail.com from your account email with a proper reason for deletion. We will review and delete your account data within 30 days.

We retain synced content while your account is active. Once deleted, content is removed from active systems (residual backups are purged on a rolling basis).

Children’s privacy

Tally is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
